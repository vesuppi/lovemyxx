Our Love Story

Tong and Xiaoxiao are a long-distance couple who have a time difference of 12 hours. They met in college and fell in love with each other immediately. However happiness didn't last long when they were young. They have been living in different countries and cultures ever since, and both have grown a lot. In 2019 they fell in love again and are determined to overcome all the difficulties to be together, to love each other, and to start a family that is full of love and support.

To my girl, and my love of life Xiaoxiao
